Railway Station and Bus Station

Athlone railway station serves the town of Athlone in County Roscommon and County Westmeath. The station is at the junction between the railway lines between Dublin-Galway and Dublin-Westport. There are three platforms, of which Numbers 2 and 3 are an island platform.

In the same place you can also find the bus station.
