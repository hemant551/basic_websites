The River Run

Tel: 07196 21777 or 087 2663866
Website: http://www.theriverrun.ie
C/o Moonriver, Main Street, Carrick On Shannon
Contact Name: Catriona McTeigue

Business Description:

Departing from the quayside beside Athlone Castle, the one hour cruise sails north into Lough Ree.
Enjoy some of the most beautiful unspoiled scenery in Ireland while our guide entertains with some live music and commentary. River Run
is ideal for day trips, corporate events, hen parties, family celebrations and wedding parties. The pleasure cruiser has been
fully refurbished to the highest standard of safety and comfort. It has a capacity of up to 60 passengers and is fully wheelchair
accessible.
River Run has a fully licensed bar on board serving tea, coffee and a full range of beverages by dedicated and fully trained staff.
